Odoo Tamara Payment Connect

Pay Securely with Tamara convenience in Odoo!

Odoo Tamara Payment Connect allows customers to make payments safely and securely on the Odoo Website. Customers only need to log in to their Tamara account to pay the product amount in installments or via the Tamara Pay later option.

Detailed Features List

Enable Tamara Payment Connect payment gateway on your Odoo website

  • Facilitates single-step integration of Tamara Payment Connect with the Odoo website.
  • The customers can pay using Tamara Payment Connect for their Odoo website orders.
  • You can enable this reliable payment gateway on your Odoo website and provide ease to your customers.

Safe and reliable payment gateway

  • The 3D secure verification is provided while paying online.
  • It is one of the safe and secure online payment gateways.
  • It offers a smooth end-to-end payment flow.

Support different cards for payment from customers

  • Tamara Payment Connect payment gateway supports various debit/credit cards supported within KSA & UAE for payment from customers.
  • Tamara Payment Connect payment gateway provides better security management using a card verification code.

Manage the Tamara Payment Connect payment method available on the Odoo website from the backend

  • From the back panel, you can easily enable/disable the Tamara Payment Connect payment method.
  • You can easily add or remove this payment gateway from your Odoo website as per your wish.

Use Tamara To Pay Product Price in Installments

  • Allows your customers to pay the product price in numerous set installments.
  • The customer can view the entire installment process via the Tamara option on the product page.

Make Payment at a Future Date via Tamara

  • The module even allows customers to pay the Product price later on via Pay Later option.
  • Customers can view the Pay Later condition on the product page.
