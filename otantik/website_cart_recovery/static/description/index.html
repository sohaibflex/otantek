Make your customers complete their purchase!!
Imagine a customer visits your website and adds products to the cart. However, due to any reason, the customer is unable to complete the checkout process. The reason behind this could be added shipping charges, change of mind or internet error, and many more. This makes the cart abandoned which is not good for the eCommerce website.
This module main functionality is to remind the customers to complete their purchases who have left products in the cart. Customers provide their contact details prior to shopping or they are the potential customers who are already registered on your website.
With their provided contact details you can send reminders via email, informing them about the products they forgot after adding them to the cart. It is a great way to recover your sales and maintain the profits on your website.
A perfect way to recover your lost sales!!
It helps you to recover the Lost Sales with the Abandoned cart recovery module. An option to select a number of follow-ups sent via email. You can make the changes to an automatic abandoned checkout recovery email. A separate list for abandoned orders is maintained to trace the email notification sent on. Cron settings are available to automatically send an email to the customer's abandoned checkout.
